Welcome!

This page allows you to add electric charges onto the universe and look at how they affect the electric field and electric potential in the universe. Make sure to explore the toolbar at the top of the screen! This project is currently a work in progress, but it's usable right now.

Note: Unfortunately, this page does not yet work under Internet Explorer. Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari, and Opera have been tested and work fine, though.
